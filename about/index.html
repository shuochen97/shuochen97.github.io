About Shuo Chen

Introdution

Hi! This is Dr. Shuo Chen. Welcome to his blog!

Shuo is currently a senior research engineer at Huawei Cloud Availability Engineering Lab. He obtained his Ph.D. in Computational Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Ya-xiang Yuan from Academy of Mathematics and System Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2024, where he was affiliated with Institute of Computational Mathematics and Scientific/Engineering Computing and State Key Laboratory of Science and Engineering Computing. Prior to this, he received B.S. in Pure and Applied Mathematics from School of Mathematics, Jilin University.
